G18 Example 1: Ensuring that a contrast ratio of at least 4.5:1 exists between text (and images of text) and background behind the text

A black background is chosen so that light colored letters that match the company logo can be used.

Source: WCAG G18

Tested on:

  • Windows 7, Firefox ESR 49.0.1, and JAWS 17
